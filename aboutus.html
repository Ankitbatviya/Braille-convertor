Our Vision

Making the world of Braille more transparent and accessible to everyone.

The Story Behind Visionary

Visionary was born from a simple idea: to demystify the Braille system for sighted individuals. Whether you are an educator preparing materials, a student learning about inclusive design, or simply curious, our tool provides an intuitive and immediate window into tactile writing. We believe that understanding is the first step toward greater accessibility and inclusion.

A Note on Grade 1 Braille

This tool focuses on Grade 1 Braille, a direct letter-for-letter substitution. It is ideal for learning and simple labels. For professional documents and books, Grade 2 Braille is used, which includes numerous contractions and shorthand rules not supported here.
